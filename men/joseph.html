🧥 Joseph

A dreamer who trusted God through every trial and became a great leader in Egypt.

📖 Life Story

Joseph was the beloved son of Jacob. God gave him special dreams about his future, but his brothers became jealous and sold him into slavery.

Although Joseph faced many hardships, including slavery and imprisonment, he remained faithful to God. God blessed him with wisdom and the ability to interpret dreams.

Eventually, Joseph became a powerful ruler in Egypt. He helped save many people from famine and later forgave the brothers who had wronged him.

🌟 Lessons from Joseph

  • ✔ Stay faithful during difficult times.
  • ✔ Trust God's plan even when life is hard.
  • ✔ Forgive those who hurt you.
  • ✔ Use your gifts wisely.
  • ✔ God can turn suffering into blessing.

💖 Key Verse

"You intended to harm me, but God intended it for good."

— Genesis 50:20
