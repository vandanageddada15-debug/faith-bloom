🏡 Martha

A devoted follower of Jesus known for her service, hospitality, and faith.

📖 Life Story

Martha was the sister of Mary and Lazarus. She welcomed Jesus into her home and was known for her hospitality and willingness to serve others.

Martha worked hard to care for her guests, but Jesus gently reminded her that spending time with Him was more important than being worried about many tasks.

When her brother Lazarus died, Martha showed remarkable faith in Jesus and trusted in His power, even during a time of great sorrow.

🌟 Lessons from Martha

  • ✔ Serve others with love.
  • ✔ Balance work and worship.
  • ✔ Trust Jesus during difficult times.
  • ✔ Have faith in God's power.
  • ✔ Make time for God every day.

💖 Key Verse

"Yes, Lord, I believe that you are the Messiah, the Son of God."

— John 11:27
