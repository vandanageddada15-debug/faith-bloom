🔥 Moses

The great leader chosen by God to deliver Israel from slavery in Egypt.

📖 Life Story

Moses was born during a dangerous time when Pharaoh ordered Hebrew baby boys to be killed. God protected Moses and he was raised in Pharaoh's palace.

Later, God appeared to Moses through a burning bush and called him to lead the Israelites out of Egypt. Although Moses felt unqualified, he obeyed God.

Through God's power, Moses led the Israelites across the Red Sea and guided them toward the Promised Land. He received the Ten Commandments and faithfully served God throughout his life.

🌟 Lessons from Moses

  • ✔ Obey God's calling.
  • ✔ Trust God even when you feel weak.
  • ✔ Lead with humility.
  • ✔ Depend on God's strength.
  • ✔ Persevere through challenges.

💖 Key Verse

"The Lord will fight for you; you need only to be still."

— Exodus 14:14
