📜 Priscilla

A faithful teacher who helped strengthen the early Church through wisdom and service.

📖 Life Story

Priscilla and her husband Aquila were devoted followers of Jesus who worked closely with the Apostle Paul.

They opened their home to believers and supported the growth of the early Christian Church through hospitality and faithful service.

Priscilla was known for her knowledge of God's Word. Together with Aquila, she helped teach Apollos and explained the message of Christ more accurately.

🌟 Lessons from Priscilla

  • ✔ Grow in knowledge of God's Word.
  • ✔ Teach others with love and patience.
  • ✔ Work together in serving God.
  • ✔ Encourage fellow believers.
  • ✔ Use your gifts to strengthen the Church.

💖 Key Verse

— Acts 18:26
