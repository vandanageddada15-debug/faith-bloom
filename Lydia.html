💜 Lydia

A successful businesswoman whose heart was opened to the message of Christ.

📖 Life Story

Lydia was a merchant who sold expensive purple cloth in the city of Philippi. She was known for her devotion to God and her hardworking nature.

When the Apostle Paul preached about Jesus, Lydia listened carefully. God opened her heart and she became one of the first believers in Europe.

Lydia welcomed Paul and his companions into her home. Through her generosity and support, she helped the early church grow and spread the Gospel.

🌟 Lessons from Lydia

  • ✔ Be open to God's Word.
  • ✔ Use your resources to help others.
  • ✔ Show generosity and hospitality.
  • ✔ Support God's work faithfully.
  • ✔ Lead by example through your actions.

💖 Key Verse

"The Lord opened her heart to respond to Paul's message."

— Acts 16:14
