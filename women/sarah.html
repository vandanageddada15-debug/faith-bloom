🌸 Sarah

A woman who trusted God's promises and became the mother of nations.

📖 Life Story

Sarah was the wife of Abraham and one of the most important women in the Bible. God promised that she would have a son, even though she was advanced in age.

At first, Sarah found it difficult to believe God's promise. Yet God remained faithful and fulfilled His word.

Sarah gave birth to Isaac, and through her family, God's covenant continued. Her story reminds us that nothing is impossible for God.

🌟 Lessons from Sarah

  • ✔ Trust God's promises.
  • ✔ Be patient and wait on God's timing.
  • ✔ Nothing is impossible for God.
  • ✔ Faith grows through challenges.
  • ✔ God always keeps His word.

💖 Key Verse

"Is anything too hard for the Lord?"

— Genesis 18:14
