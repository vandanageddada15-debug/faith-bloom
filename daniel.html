🦁 Daniel

A man of faith, prayer, courage, and unwavering trust in God.

📖 Life Story

Daniel was taken into Babylon during the exile but remained faithful to God even in a foreign land.

He refused to stop praying to God even when a law was passed against it. Because of this, he was thrown into the lion’s den.

God sent an angel to shut the mouths of the lions, and Daniel was saved without any harm.

🌟 Lessons from Daniel

  • ✔ Stay faithful even in difficult situations.
  • ✔ Never stop praying.
  • ✔ Trust God completely.
  • ✔ Stand firm in your beliefs.
  • ✔ God protects the faithful.

💖 Key Verse

"My God sent His angel and shut the mouths of the lions."

— Daniel 6:22
