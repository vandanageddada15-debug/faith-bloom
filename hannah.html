🙏 Hannah

A faithful woman whose heartfelt prayers were answered by God.

📖 Life Story

Hannah longed to have a child and poured out her heart to God in prayer. She trusted Him even during years of disappointment and waiting.

While praying at the tabernacle, Hannah promised that if God blessed her with a son, she would dedicate him to the Lord's service.

God answered her prayer, and she gave birth to Samuel. Hannah kept her promise and dedicated Samuel to God, praising Him for His faithfulness and goodness.

🌟 Lessons from Hannah

  • ✔ Pray with faith and sincerity.
  • ✔ Trust God's timing.
  • ✔ Remain patient during difficulties.
  • ✔ Give thanks for God's blessings.
  • ✔ Keep your promises to God.

💖 Key Verse

"For this child I prayed, and the Lord has granted me my petition."

— 1 Samuel 1:27
