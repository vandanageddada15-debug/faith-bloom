✝ Paul

A powerful preacher who spread the message of Jesus Christ.

📖 Life Story

Paul was once a persecutor of Christians but changed after meeting Jesus.

He traveled across many nations preaching the gospel and writing letters to churches.

His teachings form a large part of the New Testament.

🌟 Lessons from Paul

  • ✔ People can change through God.
  • ✔ Spread kindness and truth.
  • ✔ Stay strong in faith.
  • ✔ Never give up your mission.
  • ✔ Serve others with love.

💖 Key Verse

"I can do all things through Christ who strengthens me."

— Philippians 4:13

✝ Paul’s Traits

✔ Determined
✔ Brave
✔ Faithful
✔ Teacher
✔ Missionary
