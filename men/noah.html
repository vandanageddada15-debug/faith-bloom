🌊 Noah

A righteous man who trusted God and built the ark to save his family and the animals.

📖 Life Story

Noah lived during a time when people had turned away from God. Despite the wickedness around him, Noah remained faithful and obedient.

God instructed Noah to build a massive ark because a great flood was coming. Even though others mocked him, Noah trusted God and followed His commands.

Through Noah's obedience, his family and many animals were saved. After the flood, God made a covenant with Noah and placed a rainbow in the sky as a sign of His promise.

🌟 Lessons from Noah

  • ✔ Obey God even when others do not.
  • ✔ Trust God's instructions completely.
  • ✔ Remain faithful during difficult times.
  • ✔ Persevere despite criticism.
  • ✔ God's promises are always true.

💖 Key Verse

"Noah was a righteous man, blameless among the people of his time, and he walked faithfully with God."

— Genesis 6:9
