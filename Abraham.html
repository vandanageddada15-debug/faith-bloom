👑 Abraham

The father of faith who trusted God's promises and obeyed His calling.

📖 Life Story

Abraham was called by God to leave his homeland and travel to a land that God would show him.

God promised Abraham that he would become the father of many nations. Even when the promise seemed impossible, Abraham continued to trust God.

In his old age, Abraham and Sarah were blessed with a son, Isaac. Abraham's faith and obedience made him one of the most important figures in the Bible.

🌟 Lessons from Abraham

  • ✔ Trust God's promises.
  • ✔ Obey God even when the future is unclear.
  • ✔ Be patient and wait on God's timing.
  • ✔ Walk by faith, not by sight.
  • ✔ God always keeps His word.

💖 Key Verse

"Abram believed the Lord, and he credited it to him as righteousness."

— Genesis 15:6
