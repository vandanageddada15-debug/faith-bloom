⛵ Peter

A disciple of Jesus known for faith, courage, mistakes, and powerful transformation.

📖 Life Story

Peter was originally a fisherman before Jesus called him to become a “fisher of men.”

He became one of Jesus’ closest disciples and witnessed many miracles. He once walked on water toward Jesus but began to sink when he doubted.

Even after denying Jesus three times, Peter was forgiven and later became a strong leader of the early church.

🌟 Lessons from Peter

  • ✔ Faith grows through experience.
  • ✔ Even strong people can make mistakes.
  • ✔ God gives second chances.
  • ✔ Stay humble and learn.
  • ✔ Trust Jesus in every situation.

💖 Key Verse

"You are the Christ, the Son of the living God."

— Matthew 16:16
