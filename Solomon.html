👑 Solomon

A king known for his wisdom, knowledge, and dedication to God's work.

📖 Life Story

Solomon was the son of King David and became king of Israel after his father.

When God asked Solomon what he desired most, Solomon requested wisdom to lead God's people. God was pleased and gave him great wisdom, wealth, and honor.

Solomon became famous for his wise decisions and built the magnificent Temple in Jerusalem for the worship of God.

🌟 Lessons from Solomon

  • ✔ Seek wisdom above riches.
  • ✔ Ask God for guidance.
  • ✔ Use knowledge wisely.
  • ✔ Lead with understanding.
  • ✔ Honor God in all you do.

💖 Key Verse

"Give me wisdom and knowledge, that I may lead this people."

— 2 Chronicles 1:10
