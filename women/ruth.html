🌾 Ruth

A woman remembered for her loyalty, kindness, and unwavering faith in God.

📖 Life Story

Ruth was a Moabite woman who married into an Israelite family. After the death of her husband, she chose to remain with her mother-in-law Naomi instead of returning to her own people.

Ruth showed extraordinary loyalty and love, promising that Naomi's people would become her people and Naomi's God would become her God.

God blessed Ruth's faithfulness. She later married Boaz and became part of the family line of King David and ultimately Jesus Christ.

🌟 Lessons from Ruth

  • ✔ Stay loyal to those you love.
  • ✔ Trust God during difficult times.
  • ✔ Kindness makes a difference.
  • ✔ Faithfulness is rewarded.
  • ✔ God can turn sorrow into blessing.

💖 Key Verse

"Where you go I will go, and where you stay I will stay. Your people will be my people and your God my God."

— Ruth 1:16
