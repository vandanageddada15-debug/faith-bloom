✨ Mary Magdalene

A devoted follower of Jesus and the first witness to His resurrection.

📖 Life Story

Mary Magdalene was one of Jesus' most faithful followers. After Jesus healed her, she dedicated her life to serving Him.

She remained loyal to Jesus during His crucifixion when many others had fled. Her courage and devotion never wavered.

On the third day after Jesus' death, Mary Magdalene visited His tomb and became the first person to see the risen Christ. She joyfully shared the news of His resurrection with the disciples.

🌟 Lessons from Mary Magdalene

  • ✔ Follow Jesus faithfully.
  • ✔ Remain loyal during difficult times.
  • ✔ Share the good news with others.
  • ✔ Trust in God's power and promises.
  • ✔ Live with hope because Christ is risen.

💖 Key Verse

"I have seen the Lord!"

— John 20:18
