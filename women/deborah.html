⚔️ Deborah

A wise prophetess, judge, and courageous leader who guided Israel with faith and wisdom.

📖 Life Story

Deborah was one of the judges of Israel and a prophetess chosen by God to lead His people during a difficult time.

She listened carefully to God's guidance and helped settle disputes among the people with wisdom and fairness.

Deborah encouraged Barak to lead Israel's army against their enemies. Through her faith and leadership, God gave Israel victory and peace.

🌟 Lessons from Deborah

  • ✔ Lead with wisdom and courage.
  • ✔ Trust God's direction.
  • ✔ Stand firm in faith.
  • ✔ Encourage others to do what is right.
  • ✔ Use your gifts to serve God.

💖 Key Verse

"The villagers ceased in Israel; they ceased to be until I, Deborah, arose, a mother in Israel."

— Judges 5:7
