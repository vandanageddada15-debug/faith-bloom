🪕 David

A shepherd boy who became a great king and trusted God with all his heart.

📖 Life Story

David began life as a shepherd, caring for his father's sheep. Although he was the youngest of his brothers, God chose him for a special purpose.

David became famous when he defeated the giant Goliath with only a sling and a stone. His courage came from his faith in God, not his own strength.

Later, David became king of Israel. He was known as a man after God's own heart and wrote many of the Psalms found in the Bible.

🌟 Lessons from David

  • ✔ Trust God in every challenge.
  • ✔ Have courage when facing difficulties.
  • ✔ Worship God with all your heart.
  • ✔ Seek forgiveness when you make mistakes.
  • ✔ Let God guide your life.

💖 Key Verse

"The Lord is my shepherd; I shall not want."

— Psalm 23:1
