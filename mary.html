🤍 Mary

A humble and faithful woman chosen by God to be the mother of Jesus Christ.

📖 Life Story

Mary was a young woman from Nazareth who was chosen by God for a very special purpose. The angel Gabriel told her that she would give birth to Jesus, the Son of God.

Even though she did not fully understand everything, Mary trusted God and accepted His plan with humility and obedience.

Throughout Jesus' life, Mary remained faithful and devoted to God. She witnessed His ministry, His death, and His resurrection.

🌟 Lessons from Mary

  • ✔ Trust God's plan for your life.
  • ✔ Be humble and obedient.
  • ✔ Remain faithful during trials.
  • ✔ Serve God willingly.
  • ✔ Honor God in everyday life.

💖 Key Verse

"I am the Lord's servant. May your word to me be fulfilled."

— Luke 1:38
