⚔️ Joshua

A courageous leader who trusted God and led Israel into the Promised Land.

📖 Life Story

Joshua was one of Moses' faithful assistants and served God with dedication throughout his life.

After Moses died, God chose Joshua to lead the Israelites into the Promised Land. It was a huge responsibility, but Joshua trusted God's guidance.

One of Joshua's greatest victories was the fall of Jericho. By obeying God's instructions, the walls of the city came down and Israel achieved victory.

🌟 Lessons from Joshua

  • ✔ Be strong and courageous.
  • ✔ Trust God's guidance.
  • ✔ Obey God's commands.
  • ✔ Lead with faith and confidence.
  • ✔ Remember that victory comes from God.

💖 Key Verse

"Be strong and courageous. Do not be afraid; do not be discouraged, for the Lord your God will be with you wherever you go."

— Joshua 1:9
