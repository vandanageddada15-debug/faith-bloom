👑 Esther

A courageous queen who trusted God and risked her life to save her people.

📖 Life Story

Esther was a young Jewish woman who became queen of Persia. Though she lived in a foreign land, God placed her in a position of influence for a special purpose.

When an evil plan threatened to destroy the Jewish people, Esther bravely approached the king even though it could have cost her life.

Through her courage, wisdom, and faith in God, Esther helped save her people and became one of the most inspiring women in the Bible.

🌟 Lessons from Esther

  • ✔ Be courageous when facing challenges.
  • ✔ Trust God's plan for your life.
  • ✔ Stand up for what is right.
  • ✔ Use your influence to help others.
  • ✔ Faith is greater than fear.

💖 Key Verse

"And who knows but that you have come to your royal position for such a time as this?"

— Esther 4:14
